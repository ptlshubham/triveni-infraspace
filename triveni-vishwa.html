About Project

The luxurious life at Triveni Vishwa sums itself up in its line up of delightful houses set in spacious plot sizes. Each of these luxury house is a perfect combination of lavish space and exquisite style. Designed to equalize the balance of contemporary living with a need to have a private entertainment space, each house has a private terrace and a multipurpose hall on the 2nd floor which can be converted into a lounge, Home Theater, library, bar or a guest bedroom.

The spacious rooms extend from the elegant formal areas, living and dining space to the kitchen and bathrooms. Set within the safety of a gated compound wall and possessing 4 landscaped gardens with ultra modern amenities.In addition with amenities such as Concrete Road, Gated Township, 20 meter Elevated services reservoir (365x24 hours), Community Hall, Under Ground G.E.B., Morden Architectural Elevation, Temple and Decorative Main Entrance Gate has been provide.
